These two communications are an organizational-context simulation. The portfolio build was solo — there were no stakeholders to update and no design review to convene — so both samples are fictional, written in the voice of the L&D-Manager role this work targets, but grounded in the project's actual deliverables, decisions, and constraints. They demonstrate how the program would be managed and communicated inside an organization, and each carries a short note on why it is built the way it is.
